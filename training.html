Training

  • Calcutta Institute of Maxillofacial Surgery and Research has training and development mandate for the specialty as one of the prime reasons for inception.
  • Presently we offer 1 year of structured training in all wings of Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery . The trainees work as Registrars before moving on to an Associate Consultant level.
  • It is a paid residency programme in the UK pattern but in a condensed format. And a trainee joins at a level depending on clinical experience following a recognized post graduation in OMS/ENT/General Surgery.
  • Free accommodation is provided for out station trainees at or within walking distance to the institute.
  • All CMEs conducted at the institute are not only free but also mandatory for the trainees. Every trainee has to perform a fixed number of procedures (across the full spectrum) THEMSELVES, to qualify for the Certificate of Completion of Training.
